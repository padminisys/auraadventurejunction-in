About Us

Mr. Rajesh Singh, the visionary Founder and CEO of Aura Adventure Junction LLP, is an expert in the adventure activities industry, with a unique background as a certified hot air balloon pilot. Under his leadership, Aura Adventure Junction specializes in offering exciting inflatable activities and organizing large-scale adventure events. From hot air balloon rides to water zorbing and ziplining, Mr. Singh’s passion for outdoor experiences shapes every aspect of the business. With years of experience both in piloting hot air balloons and managing adventure events, Rajesh ensures that Aura Adventure Junction delivers safe, memorable, and thrilling experiences for all participants. His leadership has guided the company to become a trusted provider of inflatable attractions, catering to events ranging from corporate functions to private celebrations.
